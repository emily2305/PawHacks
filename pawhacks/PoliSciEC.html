Internships

There are many internships offered for students interested in politics, with the Senate Page Program being the most prestigious. Senate Pages are sponsored by individual Senators, so your application process is state-dependent:

Alaska — Lisa Murkowski Senate Page

New Hampshire — Maggie Hassan Senate Page

Ohio — Sherrod Brown Senate Page

Alabama — Alabama House of Representatives Page Program

Florida — Florida House of Representatives Page and Messenger Program

Georgia — Georgia General Assembly House Page Program

Summer Political Science Programs

Programs offered by colleges and universities include:

Telluride Association Summer Seminar - Location: Cornell University, University of Maryland, and University of Michigan; Length: 6 weeks; Cost: Free

Burd Institute on Media & Politics - Location: Los Angeles, CA; Length: 1 week; Cost: $1895 (in-person) or $295 (virtual)

National Student Leadership Conference on Political Action & Public Policy - Location: Washington, DC; Length: 2 weeks; Cost: $3,695

Georgetown University American Politics Academy

Emerson Politics, Activism, and Leadership Institute

George Washington University | The U.S. Intelligence Community and Counter-Terrorism

UCLA Political Science Summer Institute

Columbia Summer Immersion Program

Summer Leadership Programs

FBI NAA Youth Leadership Program - Location: Quantico, VA; Length: 6 weeks; Cost: Free (with stipend)

Bella Abzug Leadership Institute Summer Program - Location: New York, NY; Length: 2 weeks; Cost: Free

Bank of America Student Leaders Program - Location: Various internship locations, culminating in a week-long summit in Washington DC; Length: 8 weeks; Cost: Free

Subiendo: The Academy for Rising Leaders - Location: Austin, TX; Length: 5 days; Cost: Free
